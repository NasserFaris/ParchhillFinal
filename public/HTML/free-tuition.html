HOW DOES PARCH HILL HELP?

At Parch Hill we understand that both parents and schools are under immense pressure to continually raise attainment and maximise results year on year. We want to work with students from low income families to support schools and parents to increase the academic attainment of their child.

    Parch Hill provides:

  • Access to 1 to 1 support from a trained DBS checked Tutor Mentor
  • Access to our unique English and Maths resources
  • Regular progress reports to each party
  • Weekly mentoring sessions for parents
  • Inspire sessions for children
  • Study skills which helps consolidates knowledge, develops exam technique and builds confidence
  • Clear audit trail for pupil premium spend

Parch Hill is passionate about ensuring students achieve their true potential. Our goal is to help your child not only understand a subject but to also develop a knowledge about how to learn and apply their skills sets in key environments such as the home, school and work. We give students the tools they need to achieve success in all their endeavors, academic or otherwise.
